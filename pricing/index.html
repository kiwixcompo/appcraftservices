Frequently Asked Questions
h2

div

What determines if my project fits the Essential App tier?
h3

Essential Apps are perfect for well-defined projects with clear requirements, typically including basic CRUD operations, user authentication, and straightforward business logic. If your project requires complex integrations, advanced features, or extensive customization, it would fall under our Custom/Enterprise tier.

p

How long does the consultation process take?
h3

Initial consultations typically take 30-60 minutes. For Custom/Enterprise projects, we may schedule follow-up sessions to fully understand your requirements. We'll provide a detailed proposal within 3-5 business days after our consultation.

p

Do you offer ongoing maintenance after launch?
h3

Yes! All projects include 30 days of post-launch support. After that, we offer flexible maintenance packages including security updates, performance monitoring, and feature enhancements. Maintenance pricing is discussed based on your specific needs.

p

What if my project scope changes during development?
h3

We understand that requirements can evolve. For Essential Apps, significant scope changes may require moving to our Custom tier. We'll always discuss any changes with you upfront and provide transparent pricing for additional work.
